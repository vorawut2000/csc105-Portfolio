GOOD WEB DESIGN PRINCIPLES

What did I learn from how to make good web design(good web design principle). There is a lot of principles for good web design, but this four are important for me. First, purpose of your website, it is for information, entertainment, so my is information. Second, color is a well thought out colour palette can go a long way to enhance the user experience. Complementary colours create balance and harmony. Using contrasting colours for the text and background will make reading easier on the eye. And sometime its make look more modern. Third, image can tell thousand words, and choosing the right images for your website can help with brand positioning and connecting with your target audience. And finally, you can access your website to every device and look beautiful. In conclusion, these principles of effective web design can help your website be more engaging, useful, and memorable for visitors.
